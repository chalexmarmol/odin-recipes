Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is easy to make and can be enjoyed as a main dish or as a side dish. It is a hearty and filling dish that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add onion and garlic and cook until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add mozzarella cheese, milk, Parmesan cheese, parsley, and black pepper.
  7. Stir to combine.
  8. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Bake for 25 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
